About Me

Creating Cognitive Systems Where Functionality Meets Intuition

As a Senior Creative Technologist and AI Systems Designer, I build digital ecosystems that interpret, adapt, and respond intelligently. My journey began with hardware diagnostics, BIOS reprogramming, and OCR/XML workflows — disciplines rooted in precision — which I now apply to software development focused on GPT logic, conversational UX, and semantic orchestration.

I specialize in systems that behave more like collaborators than tools. They process complex inputs, understand context, and eliminate friction across workflows. From legal education and financial automation to creative prompt interfaces, my work reflects intelligent design: clarity, empathy, adaptability.

Technically, I operate with Vue, Tailwind, FastAPI, and Firebase, integrated with microservice architectures, GPT-based interaction flows, and visual-first semantics. My builds are async-ready, modular, and designed to evolve with user needs.

In all projects, I treat code as orchestration, design as cognition, and platforms as cognitive partners. I emphasize systems that reason, not react — that communicate with purpose rather than process.

Based in Zagreb, 10 000, Croatia and open to remote, hybrid, or on-site roles across Europe, the UK, and globally. Let's build technology that doesn't just function — it feels alive.
